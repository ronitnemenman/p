I have drawn to the arts since I was just 5 years old taking classes at Red Wall Art Studio. Over the past ten years, I have been able to continue practicing at various other art classes and schools, but most recently I have been at Chamblee High School. From there I have made dozens of pieces, had work in many exhibitions, and been able to help my community through art.

If you would like to contact me for commissions or inquiries on purchasing my already made works, email me at ronit.nemenman@gmail.com.
