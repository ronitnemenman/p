Echoing Thoughts

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 18 x 23.5
  • Materials:
    • Construction Paper
    • Milky Gel Pen
    • Distress Oxide
    • Paint Marker
    • Charcoal
  • Estimated Pricing:
  • Month Produced:

This piece depicts a reason of why I escape my own insecurities. When people talk about me and make comments they repeat over and over echoing and becoming louder represented by the white echoes, but their words eventutually become my own smaller thoughts as the seeds are planted in my head, shown by the much smaller echoes originating from inside the figure.

The following is a collection of process and progress photos taken during the creation of this piece along with explanations

After completing the collaging of construction paper the drawing felt empty so I added in more sound waves with a thicker paint marker
