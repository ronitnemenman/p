Exploring Escapism

Over the course of a year I explored escapism and how it affected me by answering the following questions:

  • How do I warp the world around me to escape reality?
  • What causes me to attempt to hide from the world?
  • How can I become more attentive to the world around me?
  • Will I be able to stay anchored in reality as time goes on?

These questions inspired the following collection of still lives, portraiture, and abstract works.
