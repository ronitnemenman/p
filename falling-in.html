Falling In

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 25 x 19.5
  • Materials:
    • Charcoal
    • Colored Pencil
    • Print Ink
    • Canson Paper
  • Estimated Pricing: 100$
  • Month Produced: 05/25

After a year of looking into escapism i determined, that I sould start shifting back into reality, and I hope to stay there as long as I can, but the reasons I ran in the first place haven't changed so eventually I believe I will end up falling back into my escapism. The realistic rendering of portaiture that blends into a smooth and idealistic texture for the hair shows me again begining to visible warp reality, juxtaposed on top of the almost cartoonish stamps represent the ways I have escaped becoming brighter in color as my hopes to escape again rise to the surface creates a story of falling back into escapism. The repetitive motif of juxtaposing black and white versus colored scene continues.
