Losing Control 1

Piece Information

  • Dimensions (in): 36 x 24
  • Materials:
    • Acrylic Paint
    • Black ink brush pen
    • White Paint Marker
    • Vellum
  • Estimated Pricing: 50$
  • Month Produced: idk

In an attempt to purposely give up the control and the escape I find in planning, I gave up control. First using paint scraping I put together a background completely unplanned. After I used a brush pen and simply found designs in the designs to trace and make into shapes. Next, I used the transparent substrate to my advantage to color the backs of certain sections so that when hung in the light it shines like stained glass. Finally, I made opaque sections in white and small black lines to center the piece.
