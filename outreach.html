Community Outreach

As the co president of Chamblee High School's National Art Honor Society I have been able to reach out to and volunteer in my community in many ways.

Free Art Friday

Once a month, Chamblee High School hosts a Free Art Friday in which student artists create small pieces of art to be hidden around the school to be found and kept by the rest of the student populus for free, to brighten their days and bring a touch of color to the community.
